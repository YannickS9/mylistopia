Privacy Policy for MyListopia

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyListopia. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Data Collection and Storage

MyListopia is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Storage Only: All your lists, templates, and data are stored locally on your device using Hive database.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal data to any servers or cloud services.
  • No Account Required: You can use MyListopia without creating an account or providing personal information.

3. Permissions

MyListopia may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library: To select images from your device for list items (only when you choose to use this feature).

These permissions are only used when you explicitly choose to add images to your lists. We do not access your photos or camera without your consent.

4. In-App Purchases

MyListopia offers an in-app purchase to unlock unlimited lists:

  • Purchase information is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store).
  • We do not collect, store, or process any payment information.

5. Data You Create

All content you create in MyListopia (lists, folders, templates, images) is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Under your complete control
  • Deleted permanently when you uninstall the app

6. Third-Party Services

MyListopia uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For app distribution and in-app purchase processing.

These services have their own privacy policies and we recommend reviewing them.

7. Children's Privacy

MyListopia does not collect any personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect any information, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Data Security

Your data security is important to us. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it's protected by your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, encryption).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data on any servers, you have complete control:

  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • You can manage permissions through your device settings
  • Your data never leaves your device

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store or Google Play Store, or by email at yannicksdapps@gmail.com.
